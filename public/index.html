Web Retro

How it works
  • This site runs emulators in your browser using WebAssembly via EmulatorJS (RetroArch cores).
  • No ROMs are provided. Click Select ROM to load your legally acquired ROM from your device.
  • Use the toolbar (appears over the game) for save/load state, screenshots, fullscreen, etc.
  • Settings are saved per game in your browser (IndexedDB). Clearing site data will remove them.
